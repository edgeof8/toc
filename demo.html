toc Bookmarklet Demo

This page demonstrates the toc bookmarklet that generates a Table of Contents from any webpage.

How to use:

  1. Drag the link below to your browser's bookmarks bar to create a bookmark.
  2. Navigate to any webpage with headings (like this demo page).
  3. Click the bookmarklet to generate a Table of Contents in a new tab.
